What Are We Developping?

We are developping the best and the last handheld dermotoscope you will ever use. With an integrated high-resolution camera and an IPS screen you will be able to capture your images without needing any assistance from a third party device such as a smartphone. No more buying new accesories for your dermotoscope each time you are changing to a new smartphone or giving an arm and a leg to get a tool to get your job done. We are also developping a cloud platform accompanied with state-of-the-art artificial intelligence solution to give you the metrics you need to diagnose a patient and not get in you way with you and your patient.
